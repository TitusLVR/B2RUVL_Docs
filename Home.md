# Welcome to B2RUVL

**B2RUVL** is a Blender Addon that serves as a bridge for transferring UVMaps between Blender and external UV mapping applications: **RizomUV** and **Headus UVLayout**.

## Overview

This tools is designed to streamline the workflow for 3D artists who prefer the powerful UV unwrapping tools of RizomUV or UVLayout but model and render in Blender. With a single click, you can send your mesh to the external application, perform your UV operations, and just as easily get the result back into Blender.

## Key Features

*   **RizomUV Bridge**:
    *   Seamless Send/Receive workflow.
    *   Remote control capabilities: Unfold, Optimize, Pack, Cut, Weld directly from Blender.
    *   Sync selection modes (Seams, Sharp, Crease).
    *   Support for multiple UV Sets.
*   **Headus UVLayout Bridge**:
    *   Simple Send/Receive workflow.
    *   Support for Subdivision or Poly surfaces.
    *   Option to cut based on Blender Sharp Edges.
